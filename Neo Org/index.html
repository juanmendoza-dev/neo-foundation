Our Mission

The Neo Foundation exists to inspire and empower the next generation of creators, builders, and problem-solvers. We believe that technology should be accessible to everyone — regardless of background or experience. Through hands-on workshops, mentorship, and community-driven projects, we help young people discover their potential and turn ideas into reality.
